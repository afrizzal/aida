<objective>
Build the tokenized public status page (`/status/[token]`): unauthenticated, it shows the ticket status and the PUBLIC conversation thread only (internal notes excluded at the query level), plus a reduced follow-up composer. A follow-up appends an inbound message and triggers auto-reopen (D-04). Public attachments are served through a dedicated token-scoped route that can never reach internal-note attachments.

Purpose: AIDA-08 (status link half) + the D-21/D-04 follow-up/reopen behavior. This is the v1 stand-in for email — it lets the customer see their conversation and reply without an account.
Output: status page + follow-up form + follow-up route + token-scoped attachment serve.
</objective>
